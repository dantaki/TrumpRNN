United States president, businessman, and television personality Donald Trump became widely known during the 2016 United States presidential election and his subsequent presidency for using nicknames to criticize[1] or otherwise express commentary about media figures, politicians, and foreign leaders.[a]

The list excludes commonly used nicknames such as "Mike" for "Michael" or "Steve" for "Stephen". Nicknames that are not original to Trump are annotated with footnotes.
